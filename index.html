Apartment is better than house

Why apartment is better than house?

  1. Lower Maintenance
    Apartments typically require less maintenance than houses. Landlords or property management companies often handle repairs and upkeep.
  2. Cost-Effective
    Generally, apartments are less expensive than houses, both in terms of purchase price and monthly costs like utilities and property taxes. Also it's easy to make a decision to move other place
  3. Security
    Many apartment buildings have security features, such as gated entrances and surveillance cameras, which can enhance safety.
